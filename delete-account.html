Account & Data Deletion Request

In accordance with data privacy regulations and app store policies, you have the right to request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated data from our systems.

Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your request is processed, you will lose access to your account, subscriptions, and history.

What data will be deleted?
  • Your user profile information (Name, Email, Username).
  • All personalized settings and application preferences.
  • Historical activity data and logs associated with your account.
  • Any stored third-party platform tokens and identifiers.
What data may be retained?

We only retain minimal data required to comply with legal, financial, or regulatory obligations (such as transaction invoices or fraud prevention tokens). This data will be securely isolated and never used for any marketing purposes.

How to submit your request

To initiate the deletion process, please click the button below to send us an automated email request. Make sure to send it from the email address registered with your account so we can verify your identity.

Request Data Deletion

Requests are usually processed within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once completed.
