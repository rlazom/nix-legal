Nix — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Nix ("Application") for mobile devices. The Application is designed to provide a secure and seamless user experience, and we are fully committed to protecting and respecting your privacy.

By downloading, installing, or using Nix, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

For a better experience while using our Application, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to your name, email address, and account preferences. The information that we request will be retained by us and used as described in this privacy policy.

The Application does use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These may include, depending on your device, Google Play Services, Firebase Analytics, or Apple Crashlytics.

2. Log Data

We want to inform you that whenever you use Nix, in the event of an error or crash in the app, we collect data and information (through third-party products) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol ("IP") address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our service, the time and date of your use of the service, and other statistics.

3. Cookies

Cookies are files with a small amount of data that are commonly used as anonymous unique identifiers. Nix does not use these "cookies" explicitly. However, the app may use third-party code and libraries that use "cookies" to collect information and improve their services. You have the option to either accept or refuse these cookies within your device settings.

4. Service Providers

We may employ third-party companies and individuals due to the following reasons:

  • To facilitate our Service;
  • To provide the Service on our behalf;
  • To perform Service-related services; or
  • To assist us in analyzing how our Service is used.

These third parties have access to your Personal Information only to perform the tasks assigned to them on our behalf. However, they are strictly obligated not to disclose or use the information for any other purpose.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data for as long as you maintain an active account in Nix. If you wish to permanently delete your account and all associated data, you can do so at any time via our dedicated request form available at Account & Data Deletion Request.

6. Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA & GDPR Compliance)

Nix is designed to be a family-friendly application safe for users of all ages, including children under the age of 13. We are deeply committed to protecting the privacy of our younger users.

To ensure a safe environment, we adhere to the following principles:

  • Parental Involvement: Account creation, profile setups, and any premium features or subscriptions must be managed exclusively by a parent or legal guardian.
  • No Collection of Personal Data: We do not knowingly collect, request, or store personally identifiable information (such as email addresses, phone numbers, or real full names) directly from children under 13.
  • User-Generated Content: Any stories or creative content generated within the Application is intended to be stored safely. We encourage parents to instruct their children never to include real-world personal identifiers (like last names, school names, or addresses) while creating stories.
  • Child-Safe Third-Party Services: Any third-party tools used for analytics or stability (such as Google Firebase) are configured to operate in a restricted data-processing mode that complies with COPPA and family policies, ensuring no behavioral tracking is performed on children.

If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has provided us with personal data without your supervision, please contact us immediately at the email listed below. We will take prompt action to delete such information from our records.
